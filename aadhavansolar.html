Understanding Solar Technology


1. Solar technology primarily involves the use of photovoltaic (PV) cells to convert sunlight directly into electricity.


2. These cells, typically made from semiconductor materials like silicon, capture photons from sunlight, initiating an electron flow that generates an electric current.

Key Components of Solar Systems


1. Solar Panels: These panels, composed of multiple PV cells, are the heart of any solar system. They absorb sunlight and convert it into DC (direct current) electricity.

2. Inverters: Solar inverters are crucial as they convert the DC electricity generated by solar panels into AC (alternating current), which is used to power homes, businesses, and industries.

3. Mounting and Racking Systems: These support structures securely hold solar panels in place, ensuring optimal positioning to capture sunlight efficiently.
